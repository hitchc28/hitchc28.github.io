    Welcome

    Skyscraper 1983 is a infinitely playable game where you scale a randomly-generated skyscraper, killing ghosts as you go. Play again and try to beat your old score! Will you play aggressively, and max out your score by shooting everything in your path, or use stealth to avoid danger and reach higher floors? Every play through is different!

    Contributions

  • Created an infinite-generation system to create levels.
  • Enemy and power-up spawns will be randomized.
  • Enemy strength and drop rate will scale with tower level.
  • Optimized object handling to minimize induced lag.
  • Worked with C# in the Unity3D engine.

    Gameplay

  • Run through each floor to reach new levels
  • Shoot enemies to score points
  • Shoot the ground to do a rocket jump
  • Do rocket jumps to take shortcuts and reach power-ups
