    • Mortal Life

    Unity3D Horror/Puzzle game

    ❮ ❯

    Welcome

    Explore a haunted high school to solve the mystery of what happened there! Play as Eliza, a living girl, and her best friend John, a ghost. Switch between the characters to change the environment. What exists in one world will help you solve puzzles in the other! Collect the three items, go down to the basement, and learn the shocking truth.
