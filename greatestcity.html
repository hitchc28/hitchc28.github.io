    Welcome

    In a far off land, a boy named Sully has a dream: Create the greatest city of all time. Control Sully as he explores an infinite, randomly generated world, collecting resources and recruiting villagers to help him on his journey.

    Contributions

  • Designed and implemented AI for villagers to follow commands and navigate in randomly generated world.
  • Designed and implemented quest system for tutorial and recruiting villagers.
  • Designed and implemented inventory system for player, AI, and storehouses.
  • Worked with C# in the Unity3D engine.

    Gameplay

  • Greatest City is an open-ended exploration and city building game.
  • Recruit villagers by completing quests.
  • Collect resources to recruit, build, and feed your village.
  • Play with a keyboard and mouse, or X-box controller.
  • Press A, or left-click, to take most actions (jump, talk, place...).
  • Press B, or right-click, to cancel most actions.
  • Hold RT, or E, to open the radial menu for selecting buildings to build.
  • Press Y, or Q, to open the menu system.
